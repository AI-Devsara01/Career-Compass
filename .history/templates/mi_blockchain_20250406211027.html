Role Introduction

Blockchain Developers design and implement decentralized applications (DApps) and smart contracts. They work on blockchain protocols like Ethereum, Hyperledger, or Solana to create secure, transparent, and tamper-proof systems.

Key Skills

  • Smart Contract Development (Solidity)
  • Blockchain Protocols (Ethereum, Hyperledger)
  • Web3.js, Ethers.js
  • Consensus Mechanisms
  • Security Auditing
